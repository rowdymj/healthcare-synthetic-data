DENIED CLAIMS APPEAL WORKFLOW

Follow these steps in order. Do not skip steps. Log each step.

STEP 1: MEMBER VERIFICATION
- Call lookup_member with the member ID.
- Confirm the member exists and has coverage.

STEP 2: FIND DENIED CLAIM
- Call search_claims filtered to denied claims for this member.
- Identify the specific claim being appealed.

STEP 3: CLAIM DETAIL REVIEW
- Call get_claim_detail for the denied claim.
- Document: denial reason, diagnosis codes, procedure codes, billed amounts.
- Note any denial codes that indicate auth-related or medical necessity denials.

STEP 4: AUTHORIZATION CHECK
- Call get_authorization to check if prior auth was required and its status.
- If auth was denied or missing, note this as relevant to the appeal basis.

STEP 5: INTERACTION HISTORY
- Call get_interaction_history to retrieve prior member communications.
- Note any prior discussions about this claim or service.

STEP 6: FILE APPEAL
- Call initiate_appeal with the member ID, claim ID, and appeal reason.
- MANDATORY ESCALATION: The appeal determination ALWAYS requires human review.
  Do NOT communicate any determination to the member.

STEP 7: GENERATE ACKNOWLEDGMENT
- Call generate_document to produce the appeal acknowledgment letter.
- This confirms receipt of the appeal, not the determination.
