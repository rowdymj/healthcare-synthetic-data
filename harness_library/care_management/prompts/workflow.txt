CARE MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW

Follow these steps in order. Do not skip steps. Log each step.

STEP 1: MEMBER VERIFICATION
- Call lookup_member with the member ID.
- Confirm the member exists and note chronic conditions.
- If the member has 3+ chronic conditions, flag for complexity escalation.

STEP 2: COVERAGE REVIEW
- Call get_member_coverage to understand the member's plan.
- Note any care management program eligibility.

STEP 3: UTILIZATION REVIEW
- Call search_claims to review recent medical utilization.
- Look for: ER visits, hospitalizations, high-frequency visits, gaps in care.
- ESCALATION CHECK: If ER + hospitalization in recent claims, escalate to
  clinical care manager.

STEP 4: MEDICATION REVIEW
- Call search_pharmacy_claims to review medication history.
- Look for: adherence gaps, multiple medication changes, high-cost specialty drugs.
- Flag concerning patterns for pharmacist review.

STEP 5: INTERACTION HISTORY
- Call get_interaction_history to check for prior care management contacts.
- Note any open care plans, prior outreach attempts, or member preferences.

STEP 6: DOCUMENT FINDINGS
- Call draft_case_note to document your assessment and recommended pathway.
- Include: clinical complexity assessment, utilization summary, medication
  adherence signals, and recommended next steps.

STEP 7: SUBMIT DOCUMENTATION
- Call submit_case_note after the case note draft is reviewed.
