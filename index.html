31 business rules

The healthcare logic agents need to make decisions. Click any category to see all rules.

Coverage Rules 10 rules

When is something covered? Preventive care, ER standards, referrals, OOP caps, parity.

+
COV-001
Preventive Care - Zero Cost Share
All ACA-compliant preventive services are covered at 100% with no member cost sharing when performed by an in-network provider.
If: service_category == 'Preventive Care' AND provider.network_status == 'In-Network'
Then: member_cost_share = 0, deductible_applies = false
COV-002
Emergency Room - Prudent Layperson Standard
Emergency services are covered at in-network rates regardless of whether the provider is in-network, based on the prudent layperson standard.
If: place_of_service == '23' OR claim_type == 'Emergency'
Then: apply_in_network_rates = true, regardless_of_network_status = true
COV-003
HMO Referral Requirement
HMO members must obtain a referral from their PCP before seeing a specialist. Services without referral are denied.
If: plan_type == 'HMO' AND service_category == 'Specialist Visit' AND referral_on_file == false
Then: deny_claim, denial_reason = 'Referral not obtained'
COV-004
Out-of-Network - PPO Reduced Coverage
PPO plans cover out-of-network services at reduced coinsurance. Member is responsible for balance billing.
If: plan_type == 'PPO' AND provider.network_status == 'Out-of-Network'
Then: coinsurance = plan.coinsurance_out_of_network, balance_billing_applies = true
COV-005
Out-of-Network - EPO/HMO Denial
EPO and HMO plans do not cover out-of-network services except emergencies.
If: (plan_type == 'EPO' OR plan_type == 'HMO') AND provider.network_status == 'Out-of-Network' AND NOT emergency
Then: deny_claim, denial_reason = 'Out of network provider'
COV-006
Deductible Must Be Met Before Coinsurance
For non-preventive services, the member must meet their annual deductible before the plan begins paying coinsurance.
If: service_category != 'Preventive Care' AND benefit.deductible_applies == 'After deductible'
Then: if accumulator.deductible_remaining > 0: member_pays_full_allowed, else: apply_coinsurance
COV-007
Out-of-Pocket Maximum Cap
Once a member reaches their annual OOP maximum, the plan pays 100% of covered services for the remainder of the plan year.
If: accumulator.oop_remaining <= 0
Then: member_cost_share = 0, plan_pays = 100%
COV-008
Physical Therapy Visit Limit
Physical therapy is limited to 30 visits per plan year. Additional visits require prior authorization and clinical review.
If: service_category == 'Physical Therapy' AND visits_this_year > 30
Then: require_prior_auth, if no auth: deny_claim, denial_reason = 'Exceeds benefit maximum'
COV-009
Telehealth Parity
Telehealth visits are covered at the same rate as in-person visits for the same service category.
If: service_modality == 'Telehealth'
Then: apply_same_cost_share_as_in_person_equivalent
COV-010
Mental Health Parity
Mental health and substance use disorder services must be covered at parity with medical/surgical benefits.
If: service_category IN ('Mental Health Outpatient', 'Mental Health Inpatient')
Then: cost_share_must_equal_medical_surgical_equivalent, no_separate_limits

Authorization Rules 6 rules

What needs prior auth? Inpatient, imaging, surgery, specialty drugs, DME, therapy.

+
AUTH-001
Inpatient Admission Requires Auth
All non-emergency inpatient hospital admissions require prior authorization. Emergency admissions require notification within 48 hours.
If: place_of_service == '21' AND NOT emergency
Services: Inpatient Hospital
Turnaround: Standard: 5 business days, Urgent: 24 hours, Emergency: 48 hours post-admission
AUTH-002
Advanced Imaging Auth
MRI, CT, and PET scans require prior authorization. X-rays and ultrasounds do not.
If: procedure_code IN MRI/CT/PET code ranges
Services: MRI, CT Scan, PET Scan
Turnaround: Standard: 5 business days, Urgent: 24 hours
AUTH-003
Surgical Prior Auth
Elective surgeries require prior authorization with clinical documentation supporting medical necessity.
If: procedure_code IN surgical range AND NOT emergency
Services: Outpatient Surgery, Inpatient Surgery
Turnaround: Standard: 10 business days, Urgent: 24 hours
AUTH-004
Specialty Drug Auth
Tier 4 specialty medications require prior authorization with diagnosis confirmation and step therapy documentation.
If: medication.formulary_status == 'Specialty' OR medication.avg_cost > 500
Services: Specialty Pharmacy
Turnaround: Standard: 72 hours, Urgent: 24 hours
AUTH-005
DME Prior Auth
Durable medical equipment over $500 requires prior authorization.
If: service_category == 'DME' AND estimated_cost > 500
Services: Durable Medical Equipment
Turnaround: Standard: 5 business days
AUTH-006
Extended Therapy Auth
Physical, occupational, or speech therapy beyond initial visit allotment requires authorization with progress notes.
If: therapy_visits_used >= plan.therapy_visit_limit
Services: Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy
Turnaround: Standard: 5 business days

Adjudication Rules 10 rules

How claims get processed. 10-step pipeline from eligibility through payment or denial.

+
ADJ-001
Eligibility Check
Verify member was eligible on the date of service. If not eligible, deny the claim.
If: member.eligibility_status(claim.service_date) != 'Active'
Then: deny_claim
Denial: Member not eligible on date of service
ADJ-002
Duplicate Check
Check for duplicate claims (same member, same provider, same service date, same procedure code).
If: EXISTS claim WHERE member_id = this.member_id AND provider_id = this.provider_id AND service_date = this.service_date AND procedure_code = this.procedure_code AND claim_id != this.claim_id
Then: deny_claim
Denial: Duplicate claim
ADJ-003
Timely Filing Check
Claims must be submitted within 365 days of the date of service (180 days for out-of-network).
If: days_between(claim.service_date, claim.received_date) > filing_limit
Then: deny_claim
Denial: Timely filing limit exceeded
ADJ-004
Prior Auth Verification
If the service requires prior authorization, verify an approved auth exists on file.
If: service_requires_auth == true AND NOT EXISTS approved_auth FOR this service
Then: deny_claim
Denial: Pre-authorization not obtained
ADJ-005
Network Status Pricing
Apply contracted rate for in-network, usual/customary rate for out-of-network.
If: always
Then: if in_network: allowed = contracted_rate, else: allowed = min(billed, usual_customary_rate)
ADJ-006
Apply Deductible
Apply remaining deductible to the allowed amount before calculating coinsurance.
If: benefit.deductible_applies == true AND accumulator.deductible_remaining > 0
Then: deductible_applied = min(allowed_amount, accumulator.deductible_remaining), update_accumulator
ADJ-007
Apply Copay or Coinsurance
After deductible, apply the appropriate copay (flat fee) or coinsurance (percentage) based on the benefit schedule.
If: always (after deductible)
Then: if benefit.cost_sharing_type == 'Copay': member_resp = copay_amount, else: member_resp = allowed * (1 - coinsurance_pct)
ADJ-008
OOP Maximum Check
If member has reached their OOP maximum, the plan pays 100%.
If: accumulator.oop_remaining <= 0
Then: member_responsibility = 0, plan_pays = allowed_amount
ADJ-009
COB - Coordination of Benefits
If member has other insurance, determine primary/secondary payer and adjust payment accordingly.
If: member.has_other_insurance == true
Then: apply_birthday_rule_or_employment_rule, adjust_payment_as_secondary
ADJ-010
Generate Payment or Denial
Final step: generate payment to provider or denial notice to member.
If: always
Then: if plan_paid > 0: status = 'Paid', generate_eob, else: status = 'Denied', generate_denial_notice

Pharmacy Rules 5 rules

Generic substitution, step therapy, quantity limits, specialty pharmacy, mail order.

+
RX-001
Generic Substitution
When a generic equivalent is available, the generic must be dispensed unless the prescriber specifies DAW (Dispense As Written).
If: generic_available == true AND daw_code == '0'
Then: substitute_generic, apply_generic_copay
RX-002
Step Therapy - Diabetes
GLP-1 agonists (Ozempic, Trulicity) require trial of metformin first. Must show 90-day metformin claim history.
If: medication.category == 'GLP-1' AND NOT EXISTS metformin_claim_last_90_days
Then: deny_claim, denial_reason = 'Step therapy requirement not met'
RX-003
Quantity Limits
Controlled substances and high-cost medications have quantity limits per fill.
If: medication.has_quantity_limit == true AND quantity > max_quantity
Then: deny_excess_quantity, fill_up_to_limit
RX-004
Specialty Pharmacy Mandate
Specialty medications must be filled through a designated specialty pharmacy.
If: medication.formulary_status == 'Specialty' AND pharmacy NOT IN designated_specialty_pharmacies
Then: deny_claim, denial_reason = 'Must use specialty pharmacy'
RX-005
Mail Order Discount
90-day supply via mail order pharmacy receives a cost discount (typically 2x copay for 3x supply).
If: pharmacy.type == 'Mail Order' AND days_supply == 90
Then: copay = 2 * standard_30_day_copay
